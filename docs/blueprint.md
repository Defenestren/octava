# **App Name**: OCTAVA Web Experience

## Core Features:

- Landing Page with Brand Showcase: Create an engaging landing page highlighting OCTAVA's mission, vision, and value proposition with an integrated video and clear CTAs.
- Interactive Product Catalog: Develop a filterable and searchable catalog to display OCTAVA's range of musical instruments. The AI tool assists the user in filtering the best equipment.
- Community Forum Integration: Implement a community section with blog capabilities, a forum and social media feeds, to encourage user interaction and engagement. All blogs must pass the AI tool, checking that the content follows brand guidelines.
- Dynamic Mini-Dashboard: Build a dynamic mini-dashboard connected to .xlsx files, providing key performance indicators (KPIs) for different departments (Clients, Employees, Expenses, Marketing, Products, Suppliers, Sales). Enable the upload of new data tables.
- Contact and Support System: Set up a comprehensive contact section with a contact form and direct links to support channels for customer inquiries.
- Internal Data Integration: Develop a seamless and clearly marked link to OCTAVA's intranet, enabling internal access to documents and operational data.
- About Us Section with Brand Story: Craft an authentic and engaging 'About Us' section with narratives describing OCTAVA's origins, philosophy, and commitment to the music community, optimized for SEO.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Wood Gold (#B2874D) to evoke a vintage yet elegant aesthetic, reminiscent of classic instruments. Complementing the carbon black, this adds richness and warmth.
- Background color: Carbon Black (#1F1F1F) for a sophisticated and premium feel that provides a strong contrast, enhancing readability and highlighting the brand's logo.
- Accent color: Warm gray (#A6A6A6). It maintains neutrality and modernity, allowing content and interactive elements to stand out. 
- Headline font: 'Rubik', a sans-serif font. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Body font: 'Roboto', a sans-serif font. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Maintain a clear visual hierarchy, dividing content into distinct, easily navigable sections. Use white space strategically to improve readability and reduce visual clutter.
